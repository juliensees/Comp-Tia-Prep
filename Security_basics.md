AAA  
  - Identification - making the claim of who you are  
  - Authentication - proving you are who you are  
  - Authorization - gaining access to your data  
  - Accounting - keeping track of who has authenticated  

States of Data  
  - data in transit  
      - any data being sent  
  - data at rest  
      - data being stored  
  - data in use  
      - any data being actively used by a process
   
Non-repudiation 
    - someone sending info across the internet, and the recipient knowing the source of the that original info
    - also, the person who sent it, can't deny they sent it

Gap analysis  
    - what is the org doing, and what should they be doing  
    - gap analysis allows an org to examine any deviation to plan for corrective action   

Vulnerability
    - a weakness in a system or process

Threat
    - a vulnerability that could be from a threat actor or mother nature

Risk
    - a threat with measured impact and probability 

Data sovereignty
    - when storing data, need to follow local laws/respond to legal requests
      - GDPR compliance in european union
